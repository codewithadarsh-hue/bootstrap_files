Frequently asked questions

How can I reset my account password?
Click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page, enter your registered email address, and follow the instructions sent to your email.
How do I track my support request?
Log in to your account, go to the Support section, and select My Requests to view the current status of your support tickets.
What are your customer support hours?
Our customer support team is available Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, excluding public holidays.
